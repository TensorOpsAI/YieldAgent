# Campaign Brief — Lattice DB "Lattice Cloud" Q3 Demand Gen

## Advertiser
Lattice DB — distributed analytical database for engineering teams.

## Product
**Lattice Cloud** — fully managed Lattice DB, free tier launching Q3 2026.

## Objective
Generate qualified inbound leads for the Lattice Cloud free-tier signup at
`lattice.example/cloud`. Hand-off to SDR queue.

## KPIs
- **Primary:** Cost per Marketing Qualified Lead (MQL) ≤ $120
- **Secondary:** CTR ≥ 0.6% on Sponsored Content

## Budget
$40,000 USD total, across the flight.

## Flight
2026-07-06 → 2026-08-31 (≈8 weeks).

## Audience
Senior+ engineering decision-makers at mid-market and enterprise companies in
the US, UK, Canada, and Germany. Specifically:

- **Industries:** Software Development, Financial Services, Computer Games,
  Information Technology & Services
- **Job functions:** Engineering, Information Technology
- **Seniorities:** Manager, Director, VP, CXO
- **Company sizes:** 201–500, 501–1000, 1001–5000, 5001–10000, 10001+
- **Skills:** Distributed Systems, Apache Spark, Snowflake, BigQuery, Data Engineering

**Exclude** current Lattice DB customers (CRM suppression list, handled
separately) and competitors' employees.

Three creatives in market at launch:

1. **Engineering-leader story (single image)** — VP Eng testimonial.
   - Headline: "We replaced our warehouse in 30 days."
   - Primary text: "How Northwind's data team migrated to Lattice Cloud — and what it cost."
   - CTA: Learn More
   - Landing: `lattice.example/cloud?utm_content=northwind-story`

   - Headline: "Free up to 100GB. No credit card."
   - Primary text: "Lattice Cloud's free tier is here. Spin up a warehouse in 90 seconds."
   - CTA: Sign Up
   - Landing: `lattice.example/cloud/signup`
3. **Benchmark report (single image)** — gated PDF.
   - Headline: "2026 Analytical DB Benchmark"
   - Primary text: "How Lattice Cloud, Snowflake, and BigQuery compare on TPC-H @ 1TB."
   - CTA: Download
   - Landing: `lattice.example/benchmark-2026`
