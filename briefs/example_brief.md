# Campaign Brief — Glow Roast Coffee "Midnight Brew" Launch

## Advertiser
Glow Roast Coffee — direct-to-consumer specialty coffee brand.

## Product
**Midnight Brew** — new ready-to-drink cold brew, 4-pack, launching nationwide in the US.

## Objective
Drive online sales via the Shopify store at `glowroast.example/midnight`.

## KPIs
- **Primary:** ROAS ≥ 2.5 across the flight
- **Secondary:** CPA ≤ $18

## Budget
$15,000 USD total, across the flight.

## Flight
2026-06-01 → 2026-06-30 (4 weeks).

## Audience
US-based specialty coffee drinkers, ages 25–44, urban and inner-suburban.
Skew toward existing iced / cold-brew buyers. **Exclude** existing customers
(suppression list provided separately).

## Creatives
Three creatives in market at launch:

1. **Hero video** — 15s product hero.
   - Primary text: "Slow-steeped for 18 hours. Bold without bitterness."
   - CTA: Shop Now
   - Landing: `glowroast.example/midnight`
2. **Lifestyle still** — morning-routine image.
   - Headline: "Mornings, but smoother."
   - CTA: Shop Now
   - Landing: `glowroast.example/midnight`
3. **Promo still** — first-purchase discount.
   - Headline: "20% off your first 4-pack."
   - CTA: Get Offer
   - Landing: `glowroast.example/midnight?promo=NEW20`

## Platforms
Meta (Facebook + Instagram). Future flights may extend to TikTok and Google.

## Notes
Hold the promo creative until week 2. Keep all initial drafts **paused** —
nothing goes live without approval from the marketing lead.
